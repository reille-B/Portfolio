About me

Building systems
that matter.

I'm a Software Developer with a strong data sensibility, based in the Republic of Congo. With 2 years of experience building high-stakes information systems, I've worked across fintech and national public health.

At NexCom, I developed full-stack CMMS and business dashboards end-to-end. At the Ministry of Health, I contributed to national health information systems deployed at national scale across Congo, systems where reliable data directly affects decisions that impact real lives.

That is what drives me. I don't just build features, I think in data before I think in code, because I've seen firsthand what broken data flows cost organizations and the people who depend on them.

I chose Data Engineering because it is the foundation of everything that follows, analysis, intelligence, impact. I am particularly interested in how strong data infrastructure can unlock better systems in Africa, especially in healthcare, women empowerment and development.

I am currently completing my Data Engineering training at ALX Africa, building intentionally toward that trajectory, one system at a time.
